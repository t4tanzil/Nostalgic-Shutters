Description

The Polaroid SX-70 Land Camera MINT ALPHA 1 is a highly sought-after model, known for its sophisticated design and superior performance. It retains the foldable design of the original SX-70 but includes improvements such as enhanced durability and better image quality. The manual controls offer photographers the flexibility to experiment with exposure, focus, and composition, making it a versatile tool for both creative and professional work. This camera is a collector’s dream and a reliable companion for those who cherish the art of instant photography.

  • Simple point-and-shoot camera
  • Fixed-focus lens for clear shots
  • Built-in automatic flash
  • Compatible with 600-type film
