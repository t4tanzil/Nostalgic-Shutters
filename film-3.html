Description

Kodak Portra 400 Professional is the go-to film for photographers who demand the best in color reproduction. With its ISO 400 speed, it performs beautifully in a wide range of lighting conditions, making it ideal for both studio and outdoor work. The film’s fine grain and natural skin tones make it especially popular for portrait photography, but it’s also versatile enough for landscapes and street photography. Whether you’re shooting professionally or as a hobby, Kodak Portra 400 delivers stunning results that stand the test of time.
