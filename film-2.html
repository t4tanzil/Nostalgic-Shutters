Description

Superia X-TRA FujiFilm 400 is a high-quality color film that delivers bright, sharp images with true-to-life colors. With an ISO of 400, it performs well in a variety of settings, from bright sunlight to low-light environments. The film’s fine grain and wide exposure latitude make it a popular choice for everything from portraits to landscapes. Whether you’re shooting casually or working on a serious project, Superia X-TRA FujiFilm 400 ensures that your photos come out beautifully every time.
